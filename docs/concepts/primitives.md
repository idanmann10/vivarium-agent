# Primitives

Plan, Recover, and Reflect are deliberate. Predict, Monitor, Validate, and Dream are reflexive. Execute is normal tool use coordinated by the orchestrator.

Primitives are the built-in cognitive operations that every run can rely on
before any learned skill is available. They are not world content; they are the
runtime's control flow.

## Run-Time Primitives

`Plan` chooses an approach from the goal, identity, memory, skills, traces, and
anti-patterns. `Predict` records expected outcome and confidence. `Execute`
routes provider or tool work. `Monitor` detects off-track execution. `Recover`
narrows, replans, escalates, or aborts. `Validate` scores the result and records
reasons. `Reflect` captures lessons and publishable candidates.

## Background Primitive

`Dream` runs outside the immediate goal loop. It consolidates memory, promotes or
prunes skills, extracts anti-pattern and trace candidates, updates identity,
refreshes habituation, and recalibrates confidence.
